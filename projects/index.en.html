PiClass Projects

Entre-Bois - Lausanne (CH)

We have a PiClass pilot project at the primary school of Entre-Bois in 4 classes with kids from 6 to 10 years old. The students will have 1 lesson each week starting in September 2014 and ending in July 2015. Which gives us 38 class lessons for a total of 152 lessons in the school year.

To improve the learning environment the classrooms are split in 2 and provide 10 workplaces. Each student will benefit from the workshop for half of the school year. This means 19 lessons per student.

The students will learn about electronics, robotic and programming through 8 different activities and create a website about their work. They will also train in sciences, mathematics, creativity and discover new technologies. Here is the activities plan giving us 2 free lessons and individual projects:

  • Using a Raspberry Pi: 1 lesson
  • My desktop and my documents: 1 lesson
  • First steps in programming: 2 lessons
  • The colored leds: 2 lessons
  • Weather station: 2 lessons
  • The 8x8 matrix leds: 2 lessons
  • Developing a game with scratch: 4 lessons
  • The music box: 2 lessons
  • The web site: 1 lesson
