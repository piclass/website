Entre-Bois - Lausanne (CH)

We have a PiClass pilot project at the primary school of Entre-Bois in 4 classes with kids from 6 to 10 years old. The students will have 1 lesson each week starting in september 2014 and ending in jully 2015. Wich give us 38 lessons by classes for a total of 152 lessons on the school year.

To improve the learning environment the classrom are cut in 2 and provide 10 workplace. Each student will benefit the workshop for the halt of the school year. This means 19 lessons by student.

The students will learen about electronics, robotic and programing through 8 activities and the creation of a website about their work. They will also train sciences, mathematics, creativity and discover new thechnologies. Here is the activities planning letting us 2 free lessons for free and individual projects:

  • Using a Raspberry Pi: 1 lesson
  • My desktop and my documents: 1 lesson
  • First step in programing: 2 lessons
  • The colored leds: 2 lessons
  • Wheather station: 2 lessons
  • The 8x8 matrix leds: 2 lessons
  • Developing a game with scratch: 4 lessons
  • The music box: 2 lessons
  • The web site: 1 lesson
