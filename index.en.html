The PiClass Project

Raspberry Pi Travelling Classroom - The project goal is to provide a travelling computer lab with some robotics components using a Raspberry Pi. The classroom can support half a class, ie from 10 to 12 students and will travel between school buildings in a suitcase on wheels!

Check out our Twitter feed for latest news about the project: @pi_class and you may also be interested by some informations about our active projects.

Who is it for?

In the first round, the project is designed for primary Swiss classes located in the French speaking part of Switzerland. The workshop activities are also available for non profit associations working with young kids and teens.

In the second round, we will provide our services all over the world between July and August. Depending on the communty growth and needs, we will provide some instructions to teachers and help to setup a local PiClass lab.

How much it will cost ?

The Raspberry Pi micro computer and the electronics components we selected are low cost, energy efficient and were created for educational purposes. A single machine costs only $35, it lets us have a standard graphical desktop with educational applications that can play with robotics, create electronics circuits and teach programming.

First we will start with a Kickstarter fundraising. Then we will need an annual budget to pay volunteers and reparations, this will cost around $350 a year.
